CL24~C5~P2~BN~D1 -> F -> D256 -> D3
============================
CL24 -> convolutional layer with 24 filters
5 -> kernel_size
P2 -> max pooling
BN -> batch normalization
D1 -> 1% dropout
F -> flatten
D256 -> dense layer with 256 units
